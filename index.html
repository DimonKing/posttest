Thinking about selling your business?

My name is Michael Polyakov and I'm a Slovenian based private investor, working predominantly with small and medium business owners, looking to sell their business, raise funds or expand to global markets.

Your business can mean a lot to you and my job is to find the best solution that would give you more freedom to achieve your goals.

What solutions do I offer?

Exit Strategy

seling your business

If you have a solid profitable business but for some reason you're looking to sell it, I would gladly discuss the deal if your business meets the following criteria:

  • ​The business has been working for over 5 years
  • It has revenue over 500,000 euros
  • You haven't advertised your business with online brokers

Becoming global

Attracting global customers

If your company is well established and profitable, and you feel like it has the potential of attracting more business on a larger scale, then we can discuss the ways you can raise funds and expand your business globally. The requirements are as follows:

  • The business has been working for over 5 years
  • Annual profit of over 500,000 euros
  • You're not looking to exit the company and you're willing
  • to expand to global markets

Companies in distress

Taking the pressure off

If your company is under financial distress, I would be glad to go over the details and see if there is a potential as long as your company:

  • Has been running for over 5 years
  • Has revenue over 500,000 euros
